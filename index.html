ABOUT NYOTA PROJECT

The Government of Kenya, through a strategic collaboration involving the Ministry of Youth Affairs, the State Department for Youth Affairs and the Creative Economy, and the Micro and Small Enterprises Authority (MSEA), is pleased to announce the official Call for Applications for the NYOTA Project – Cycle 1 (2025).

The National Youth Opportunities Towards Advancement (NYOTA) Project is a transformative government-led initiative designed to provide young people with opportunities for self-reliance, skills development, business support, and sustainable income generation. NYOTA represents a commitment to youth empowerment, especially among vulnerable and underserved segments of the population. This countrywide call targets young Kenyans interested in receiving business support, including training, mentorship, apprenticeship placements, and business grants, with the goal of fostering a culture of entrepreneurship, financial literacy, and self-sufficiency.



The NYOTA Project seeks to:

  • Increase employment opportunities for you.
  • promote entrepreneurship and provide business development support.
  • Offer skilling and apprenticeship programs.
  • Instill a culture of saving and financial literacy among youth.
  • Provide inclusive access for youth living with disabilities and those from marginalized communities.

    • ☆*Through this initiative, the government aims to reduce youth unemployment and empower the next generation of entrepreneurs, artisans, and skilled workers across the country.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Be a Kenyan citizen
  • Be aged between 18 and 29 years.
  • For youth living with disabilities, the age limit is extended to 35 years.
  • Possess a valid Kenyan National Identity Card (ID).
  • Own a SIM card that is registered in your name and linked to your National ID.

Step-by-Step Application Instructions:

  1. On your mobile phone, dial the USSD code *254#.
  2. From the options displayed, select the “NYOTA Project”.
  3. Follow the prompts and complete the application
  4. Ensure your details match those on your National ID and SIM registration records.


Application Deadline

Deadline for submission is: 15th May 2025 (11:59 PM) Late applications will not be accepted, so we encourage all interested applicants to apply well before the deadline to avoid last-minute issues
